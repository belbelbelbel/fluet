- [ ] Royalty-free license
- [ ] Commercial use allowed
- [ ] No watermarks or branding
- [ ] Good visual quality (not pixelated)

## ğŸ” Where to Find Assets

### Free Audio Sources
1. **FreeSound.org** (https://freesound.org)
   - Free account required
   - Search by tags: "rain", "ambient", "white noise"
   - Filter by license: "Attribution" or "CC0"

2. **Pixabay Audio** (https://pixabay.com/music/)
   - Free, no attribution required
   - High quality
   - Easy to download

3. **Zapsplat** (https://www.zapsplat.com)
   - Free account available
   - High quality
   - Commercial use allowed

### Free Video Sources
1. **Pexels Videos** (https://www.pexels.com/videos/)
   - Free, no attribution required
   - High quality
   - Easy to download

2. **Pixabay Videos** (https://pixabay.com/videos/)
   - Free, no attribution required
   - Good variety

3. **Videvo** (https://www.videvo.net)
   - Free account available
   - Check license for each video
